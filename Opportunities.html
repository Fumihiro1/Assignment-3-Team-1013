Opportunities with the online learning topic.

There were many opportunities in the said topic regarding online learning. One of them is the future of learning moving forward. Although traditional onsite learning has its obvious benefits, it may not be sustainable in the future. Online learning can still provide good quality learning, and with the large population of students siding towards the virtual learning environments, developers all around the world will have their hands tied to creating great learning applications.

Students will no doubt be exposed many opportunities as well moving forward with the topic of online learning. According to ccaurora.com, the time flexibility online learning can offer means that students do not need to be tied to a traditional 7am – 3pm time schedule, which "most" schools tend to have. There can be more suitable resource / assignment due dates as well, considering that the resources and assignments do not need to be handed in via a hard copy, it can make it easier for material to be gathered from students. ​

Online learning provides a more efficient way for teachers to deliver lectures, online learning can be delivered in multiple ways such as, video, word doc, pdf or live videos. It will also save the time for teachers and students on the road, teachers can deliver lessons at home and students can attend lectures anywhere else instead of being restricted by geographical boundaries.

Online learning reduces financial cost for students and the teachers, it is far more affordable than attending lectures, especially to those who live in rural areas, other than waking up before the sun does, transportation fee is a headache. This will decrease the rate students miss out the lesson, the attendance overall has been improved.

When viewing the importance of online learning, the flexible learning hour is a big factor. Online learning offers students a chance to learn whenever the location or time suits them. Considering different learning habits each student has, the traditional class forces students to keep on the pace of the class, otherwise they will be left behind, online learning leaves students more space to and more freedom to study at their own space.
