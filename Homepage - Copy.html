Online Learning: The impacts of staying virtual.

Due Date: 12/06/2022 - Calender Week 12
Chosen Topic: Online Learning

The topic we chose to investigate in our extensive research is Online Learning. Online learning is a form
of virtual study where students are taught via online sources such as Canvas and Zoom. One of the
main reasons why Online Learning has been a global interest for a few years now is due to the global
pandemic which started occuring during the first few months of 2020, known as Covid-19. This
effectively put many students and teachers into a lockdown state, where only bare essentials were
able to be open like a supermarket or a pharmacy, to stop the spread of the virus. This meant that students
needed a new form of education to partake in during the various lockdowns the globe has encountered,
which raised the attention to Online / Virtual Learning. The reason why we found this topic to be
interesting to us is because we have been taking part in online learning ourselves, at AUT.
Most classes at the start of 2022 at AUT were online, with the exception of some being changed
to being held on campus after the Pandemic started dying down. We thought that this extensive
research opportunity for an assignment was great to find out the different effects of online learning
has on students, as being students this research could help us gain a deeper understanding of what
online learning can do to us, but also the future moving forward with online learning.
